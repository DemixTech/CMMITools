Personnel

Risk Description

One of the appraisal team members may become ill or unavailable during the appraisal.

Risk Impact

Low

Risk Mitigation

1) Excluding the LA, plan for 4+ ATMs. 2) LA will work as an ATM if required. 3) Setup the schedule so that all appraisal team members are in all affirmation interviews.

2) Virtual Tool Failure

Risk Category

Virtual technology

Risk Description

DVS (Demix virtual services) tools may become unavailable due to network failures.

Risk Impact

High

Risk Mitigation

1) Ensure that backup network facilities are available. 2) Ensure that additional overtime is planned and agreed upon should technical difficulties cause appraisal work disruptions. 3) Conduct a test of virtual technologies before the opening briefing. 4) Use Voov as a backup technology. 5) Setup WeChat and WhatsApp groups for backup communication when a failure occurs (between ATL, ATMs, RI and Site-coordinator)

Risk Status

Closed

Risk Category

Virtual Delivery

Risk Description

Potential Confidentiality breaches could happen when participant meetings and ATM meetings happen when using Virtual Delivery methods.

Risk Impact

High

Risk Mitigation

1) Separate interview meetings, appraisal team meetings and presentations [van Zyl, Pieter] 2) The ATL (Appraisal Team Leader) will control access to the above meetings to ensure Confidentiality is maintained [van Zyl, Pieter] 3) All meetings will be scheduled with passwords to assist to control access to the meetings, assisting that Confidentiality is maintained [van Zyl, Pieter]
